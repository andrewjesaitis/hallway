Why use Hallway?

Developing social connections in the classroom (online or offline) is critical to student success. It allows students to explore knowledge safely, helps groups become cohesive, and allows for nuanced feedback that helps students improve. With the advent of high speed internet connections, online classrooms need not suffer from the poverty that rich communication provides. Hallway provides an asynchronous link, so you can get to know your classmates personally.

Helps all types of students

All students can benefit from increased social presence. Researchers have found that extroverts sometimes find it easier to express themselves via speech than writing so they generally prefer video over text based participation. Introverts liked the ability to record their considered thoughts without fear of being drowned-out in a traditional classroom. Finally, the English language learner found it helpful to practice a different set of communication skills.[1]
